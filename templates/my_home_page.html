Speech Synthesis

This is the generation, creation or synthesis of speech in another language. In this project, English and Yoruba are the source and target languages respectively. The main focus of this work is to develop English-Yoruba parallel corporal for speech synthesis. To assist in the future research work in speech processing, recognition, and synthesis, the coproral to be curated in this project would be publicly made available for research purposes.

Speech synthesis can either be from texts or speeches (this project). For speech-to-speech synthesis, parallel speech corporal of both the speaker language (source language) and the responder language (target language) are utilised to train AI model developed for the sole purpose of training the data to obtain state-of-the-art results. However, African Indigenous languages are Low Resourced in nature meaning there are insufficeints speech corporal to train AI models. Hence, the reason for drawback or lack of development in the speech processing applications for African languages unlike the prevalence of these technologies for the High Resourced Languages.

About

This web page has been developed to collect parallel accented English-Yoruba speech corporal for speech synthesis research work. The aim is to obtain naturally accented corporal of both English and Yoruba, and these corporal are to be made available for public use for research related to speech synthesis.
Crowdsourced individual shall participate in recording their voices using this platform. Three pages will be utilised for this tasks. These are the Transcript page, Yoruba Corpus page and English Corpus page. The Transcript page is to link you to the storage location of the transcripts. The Yoruba Corpus page is the page where you record your voice in Yoruba language. The English Corpus page is where you record your voice in English language. You can also navigate these pages using the links on the top-left of this page.
The recording exercises shall span a period of 5 working days from Monday to Friday.
To participate in the recording activities, participants are to register their details using the link beneath the video on the right side of this page.

To ensure participants have the best experience in this recording exercises, a feedback form has been made available to know what improvement to make for the remaining days of this exercises-this can be found under the registration link.
